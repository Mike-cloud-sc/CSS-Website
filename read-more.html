I lived in Maumee, Ohio most of my life. My wife Armeda and I now reside in a retirement community called Sun City in Georgetown, Texas. We attended St. Joseph's elementary and Maumee High School together and started dating at age 16. We will be celebrating our 57th anniversary this year (2021). We have two sons(Mike Jr. and David), two granddaughters(Emma and Sophia) and one grandson(Nicholas). Except for programming, we have the same hobbies, including cooking, and pretty much do everything together. We have learned how to play Pinochle and Euchre online and really enjoy playing with friends and family.
